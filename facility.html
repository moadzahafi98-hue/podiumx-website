A Facility Built for Elite Performance

245 m² of purpose-built training, conditioning, and recovery zones equipped with the tools athletes trust.

Facility Zones

Every Area Has a Purpose

PodiumX is split into four specialized zones to support every moment of your training journey.

Training Zone • 100 m²

Eleiko barbells, squat racks, bumper plates, kettlebells, sled track, plyometric boxes, and specialty bars for Olympic lifting, strength, and power development.

Cardio & Reception • 100 m²

Rowers, assault bikes, ski-ergs, treadmills, and a community lounge to prep, cool down, or connect with fellow athletes.

Recovery Area • 30 m²

Glass sauna, dual ice baths, mobility tools, and recovery bench to keep your nervous system primed and your body resilient.

Locker Rooms • 15 m²

Premium showers, secure lockers, grooming stations, and daily towel service so you can transition smoothly to your next commitment.

Tech & Tools

  • Heart-rate and velocity-based tracking options
  • Force plates available for performance testing
  • Mobility and prehab station with bands and rollers
  • Dedicated warm-up and cooldown protocols

Member Experience

  • Concierge check-in and training log support
  • Community area with espresso bar & lounge
  • Monthly workshops with guest coaches
  • Secure parking and easy city access
