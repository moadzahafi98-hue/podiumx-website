How does the free evaluation work? +
You’ll meet with a PodiumX coach for a movement assessment, goal setting discussion, and training roadmap. Expect around 45 minutes and come ready to move.
Are there limits on group session sizes? +
Yes. To ensure coaching quality, we cap small-group training at 10 athletes per session.
Can I pause my membership? +
Memberships can be paused for up to 30 days per year for travel or injury. Just notify the front desk at least 5 days in advance.
Do you offer corporate or team programs? +
Absolutely. We create custom programs for corporate teams and sports clubs. Contact us with your requirements and timeline.
What recovery services are included? +
Members can add the recovery package which includes unlimited access to ice baths, sauna, mobility tools, and coach-led regeneration protocols.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, PodiumX provides secure parking for members during session hours.
